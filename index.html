Product

Inspired by the free and passionate woman who became Coco Chanel, GABRIELLE CHANEL ESSENCE is a more voluptuous, intensely feminine interpretation of the original GABRIELLE CHANEL luminous floral fragrance. The striking square glass bottle is reimagined with bolder golden accents on the stopper and label.

Composition

Composed by In-House Perfumer Creator Olivier Polge, this deep and enveloping scent evokes the true essence of Gabrielle Chanel. The fragrance is an imaginary bloom, faceted with jasmine, ylang-ylang, orange blossom and the most captivating flower of all: Grasse tuberose. Solar and radiant with a warm trail, it is the scent of a woman who follows her own path.
